Why the Current Model Falls Short

What EO Has Done

Evaluated multiple third-party platforms:

  • • StormGeo

  • • Sofar Ocean
  • • Coach Solutions
  • • ZeroNorth

What We Learned

Each platform had strengths, but:

  • Platforms are built generically
  • Customization is constrained
  • EO workflows are retro-fitted, not embedded

Resulting Reality

  • Manual overrides become routine
  • "Grey-box" decisions emerge
  • Trust, compliance, and accountability erode over time

This is a structural limitation — not a vendor execution failure.
